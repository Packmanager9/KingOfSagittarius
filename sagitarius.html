King of Sagittarius

  • Click a colored button to pick a species
  • Humans are blue
  • Buggos are green
  • Kursa are orange
  • Vaptrons are pink
  • Press the white button to colonize while using a colony ship
  • It costs 500 minerals to build a colony ship or a skirmisher
  • The red button builds colony ships when you select one of your planets
  • The purple button builds skirmisher ships when you select one of your planets
  • The yellow button can repair ships at your planets
  • It is cheaper to repair than to rebuild
  • Spacebar is a shortcut to move a ship to a planet
  • the C key is a shortcut to repair a ship
